Roller Garden offers the best skating experience in town!

Rolling fun for everyone! Minnesota's #1 roller skating rink, for 75 years this building and location have provided skating entertainment for people of all ages (49 years as Roller Garden). The beautiful hard wood maple skating floor, super sound system, friendly customer service, exciting redemption games, tempting snackbar, awesome souvenir selections and the wide variety of skates for sale combine to make an incredible entertainment experience.

At Roller Garden, we pride ourselves by providing a clean, safe, friendly and fun experience for your entire family. We offer roller skating and roller blading for all ages! We specialize in birthday parties, private group events, school field trips, fundraisers and adults only sessions.

Roller Garden is family owned, operated and staffed by people who care about our customers. We consider every skating group "A Party!"
